# FinVault — Investment Intelligence Hub

A sleek, client-side financial toolkit for long-term investors. No accounts, no APIs, no tracking — just smart tools that run entirely in your browser.

## What's Inside

**Compound Interest Engine** — Model how your wealth grows over time with yearly compounding. Set your principal, monthly contributions, annual rate, and time horizon. Includes a full year-by-year breakdown table.

**Inflation Erosion Calculator** — See how inflation silently destroys your purchasing power. Features a "half-life of money" metric showing when your money loses half its value.

**Investment Goal Planner** — Enter your financial target and find out exactly how much you need to save monthly (and daily) to get there. Factors in starting capital and expected returns.

**World Market Indices** — A curated reference of 12 major indices across the US, Europe, and Asia — S&P 500, Dow Jones, NASDAQ 100, Russell 2000, FTSE 100, DAX, CAC 40, Euro Stoxx 50, Nikkei 225, Hang Seng, Shanghai Composite, and KOSPI. Each with descriptions, founding year, component count, and mini performance charts. Filterable by region.

All three calculators support **8 currencies**: USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CHF, CAD, AUD, and PLN.

## Getting Started

It's a single HTML file. No build steps, no dependencies, no setup.

```
git clone https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/finvault.git
open finvault.html
```

Or just download `finvault.html` and open it in any browser.
